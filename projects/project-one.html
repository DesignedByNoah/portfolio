Project One

Vicenza, Italy · Museum · 2025

Problems: Design next to Andrea Palladio’s Palladiana Basilica in the heart of Vicenza, Italy. Lack of good places to find shade in the city.
Solution: Placing the museum underground stands down to the grand architecture of Palladio. Large park above the museum, and the galleries themselves offer places for the community to gather in cooler spaces during the rising heat years.
